About SeekHealing

SeekHealing is a non-profit, non-partisan project organization. Our mission is to help people feel safe enough and connect deeply enough to catalyze transformational, sustainable healing from opioid addiction.

To accomplish this, the project creates the conditions for symbiotic relationships to occur organically between lonely or disconnected subcultures and individuals.

Older Adults who experience disconnectedness in retirement

Adults with Opiate Bonds

Adults in recovery, or seeking next step in personal growth

Younger adults who want to make a difference & feel a lack of purpose

We connect people or groups of people in order to facilitate measurable improvements to health & happiness to all parties. By rejecting the medicalization model of therapist-patient relationships, we can capitalize on the natural byproducts of community interdependence where people get better by helping each other. This approach drives permanent changes to addiction patterns while also promoting the health & happiness of volunteers.
